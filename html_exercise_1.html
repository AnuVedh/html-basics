Sri Rama Navami

Ram Navami is a famous Hindu festival. This festival is celebrated in order to honour the birth of Marayada Purshottam Ram, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu.
This day also known as Sri Rama Navami marks the end of nine-day Chaitra-Navratri celebrations.
The festival of Ram Navami is held in high regard not only by the people in India but also by the Hindu community residing in the other parts of the world. The festival is celebrated with extreme joy and excitement. Many devotees also fast on this occasion.
It is considered that all the devotees who fast on this day are showered with endless happiness and good luck by Lord Rama. Read on, if you want to explore the history of Ram Navami.
